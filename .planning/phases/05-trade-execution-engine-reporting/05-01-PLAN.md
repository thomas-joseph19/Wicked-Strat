**Sizing (EXEC-03):**  
`full_size = max(1, floor((account_size * risk_per_trade) / (abs(entry - stop) * point_value)))`  
then `effective = max(1, floor(full_size * setup.position_size_scale))`.

**Touch rules (long):** TP1 at `high_nq >= tp1`; SL at `low_nq <= stop` (initial stop); TP2 at `high_nq >= tp2` in OPEN_PARTIAL; breakeven at `low_nq <= entry` after TP1. **Short:** mirror with highs/lows swapped.

**First risk bar (Claude discretion locked in PLAN):** Begin intrabar TP/SL evaluation on bars with **index > setup.created_at** only (subsequent bars after entry bar close), to avoid lookahead on the entry bar itself. Entry fill still occurs when transitioning into `OPEN_FULL` on the first processed bar after `created_at` at **`setup.entry_price`** (limit model consistent with D-04).

**OPEN_PARTIAL conflict (05-RESEARCH):** On same bar, evaluate **TP2 touch before breakeven SL** (symmetry with D-05).
